It is recommended that schools purchase a MyGolf School Kit from the MyGolf Shop - www.mygolf.org.au

A Kit is designed so that a group of twenty players can participate with one club between two players and will have the following items:

  • 10 x 7-Irons (8 right handed and 2 left handed)
  • 10 x Putters
  • 10 x Hitting Mats
  • 100 x PVC Golf Balls
  • 10 x Putting Targets
  • 1 x Kit Bag

ALTERNATE EQUIPMENT

Other existing equipment that can be utilised to run the MyGolf Sporting Schools Program could include:

  • Tennis Balls
  • Witches Hats
  • Hula Hoops
  • Ropes
  • Flags
  • Chalk and Masking Tape
  • Football Goals
