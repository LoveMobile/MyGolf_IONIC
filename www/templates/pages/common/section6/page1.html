How To Play

  • Nominate a player to lead.
  • The leader demonstrates a skill (kicking, passing, shooting, throwing, etc).
  • Starting on the leaderâ€™s right, each player in turn demonstrates the skill around the circle.
  • When it reaches the leader once more, they introduce another skill.

Additions

  • Next time around, the leader introduces a new skill when the person opposite them in the circle demonstrates the original skill. Each time, introduce a new skill earlier.
  • The next player in the circle repeats the movement and adds a movement of their own. The next player adds another movement and so on. Each player needs to remember the previous pattern.

Equipment

  • Cones / Witches Hats

Safety

  • Make sure there is enough space between players to avoid contact.
