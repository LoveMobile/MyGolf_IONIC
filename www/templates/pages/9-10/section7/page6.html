Outline

  • Split students into pairs or small groups.
  • Students attempt to chip the ball into the circle surrounding the hole.
  • 10 points are scored for finishing inside the circle; 5 points if the ball rolls through the circle.

Equipment

  • Rope / Cones
  • Tennis Balls / PVC Balls
  • Hitting Mats

Set Up

  • Place rope or markers in a circle around the ‘hole’ approximately 20 metres from the hitting station and 5 metres in diameter.
  • Create a safe zone for students to sit behind the hitting station.

Purpose

  • Aim and alignment
  • Distance Control
  • Impact
