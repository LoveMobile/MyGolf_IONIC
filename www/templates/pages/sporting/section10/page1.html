Throughout the MyGolf Sporting Schools Program there are many opportunities for students to take on a leadership role. This could be done through the appointment of students in various roles.

These roles can either be maintained for the duration of the program or rotated between different students each session to allow more students to experience these roles.

Some examples of the roles and responsibilities include:

Team Captains

  • Select and coordinate teams for activities.
  • Assist and encourage team members.
  • Promote and monitor safety procedures.
  • Set rules and assist enforcing them during sessions.

Equipment Managers

  • Retrieve and return equipment from storage.
  • Setup and pack up session area.
  • Clean equipment and inspect for damage.

Fitness Leaders

  • Lead students through warm up games, activities and stretching exercises.

Scorers

  • Assist with the scoring within games, activities and competitions.
  • Create scoring zones and inform group what points are on offer.

Course Designers

  • When delivering “design your own course” activity students can design the course layout used for the competition.
  • Provide a map of the course layout.
