LESSON LEARNING INTENTION

To introduce the elements of safety, grip, stance and swing through the delivery of minor games and activities.

LESSON PLAN

Skill Description Activity/Game Time
Warm Up Stretching & Fun Aerobic Game 10 mins
Introduction Introduction to Golf and Safety Aspects 15 mins
Chipping Bulls Eye Golf 15 mins
Full Swing Footy Golf 15 mins
Review What did you learn today? 5 mins

Curriculum Band Summary

By completing this lesson you will be covering the following content descriptors through Chipping and Full Swing activities.

Sub-strand 1: Moving Our Body

  • Perform and refine specialised movement skills in challenging movement situations.

Sub-strand 2: Understanding Movement

  • Analyse the impact of effort, space, time, objects and people when composing and performing movement sequences.

Sub-strand 3: Learning through movement

  • Transfer understanding from previous movement experiences to create solutions to movement challenges.

Equipment

  • Markers
  • Tennis Balls
  • Hoops
  • Large Rope

Time

45-60 mins

Area

Oval / Gym

Focus Questions

  • What skills have you previously learnt from other sports that would be helpful in golf?
  • What variables change in the swing between putting, chipping and a full swing?
