Outline

  • Split students into pairs or small groups
  • Students attempt to hit the ball and carry the river
  • 10 points are scored if attempt is successful
Level / Difficulty Equipment River Width River Distance
Level 1 Tennis Balls 3 metres 20 metres
Level 2 PVC Golf Balls 3 metres 20 metres
Level 3 PVC Golf Balls 3 metres 25 metres

Equipment

  • Witches Hats
  • Tennis Balls / PVC Golf Balls
  • Hitting Mats

Set Up

  • Place witches hats or cones to act as the ‘River’ approximately 15 metres. (distance can be adjusted based on student skill level)
  • Create a safe zone for students to sit behind the hitting station.

Purpose

  • Aim and alignment
  • Distance Control
