LESSON LEARNING INTENTION

To allow children to implement the use of all the skills of putting, chipping and full swing within playing a game of golf on a course they have designed.

LESSON PLAN

Skill Description Activity/Game Time
Warm Up Stretching & Fun Aerobic Game 10 mins
Course Design Create Design for course 15 mins
Putting / Chipping / Full Swing Play the Course 30 mins
Review What did you learn today? 5 mins

Curriculum Band Summary

Completing this lesson you will be covering the following content descriptors by participating in a class golf competition.

Sub-strand 1: Moving Our Body

  • Practise, apply and transfer movement concepts and strategies.

Sub-strand 2: Understanding Movement

  • Demonstrate and explain how the elements of effort, space, time, objects and people can enhance performance.

Sub-strand 3: Learning through movement

  • Evaluate and justify reasons for decisions and choices of action when solving movement challenges.

Equipment

  • MyGolf Kit
  • Markers
  • Tennis Balls
  • Hoops
  • Large Rope

Time

45-60 mins

Area

Oval / Gym

Focus Questions

  • When playing your shots, when was it best to hit the ball in the air?
