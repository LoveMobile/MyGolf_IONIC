Outline

  • Students can work as individuals or as part of teams.
  • Students take it in turns to hit as far as they can.
  • Points are awarded for each flag that they hit past: 1st flag = 10 points 2nd flag = 50 points 3rd flag = 100 points
  • Introduce the “fairway” and demonstrate that the balls need to land inside the cones to count and receive points.
  • An individual game can be conducted to award the longest drive.
Level / Difficulty Equipment Distance to Flags Width of Fairway
Level 1 Tennis Balls 20m, 40m, 60m none
Level 2 Tennis Balls 20m, 40m, 60m none
Level 3 PVC Golf Balls 20m, 40m, 60m 40-50m

Equipment

  • Sticks, witches hats and cones
  • Three flags
  • Hitting Mats

Set Up

  • Place targets (e.g. a flag) certain distances out from the hitting areas, e.g. 30, 50m & 70m.
  • Create a “V” or “fairway” down the driving range using cones (optional).

Purpose

  • Aim and alignment
  • Distance Control
